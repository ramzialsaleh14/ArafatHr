Arafat HR — Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 3, 2025

Thank you for using Arafat HR. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes the data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Summary

  • The app collects minimal personal information required to provide HR features.
  • We store data securely and use third-party services only as needed. We do not sell your data.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account data: name, user ID, email/username, password.
  • Notes and attachments you upload inside the app.

2.2 Device & Usage Data

  • Crash logs and analytics to improve the app.
  • Device information such as model, OS, and app version.

2.3 Location Data

  • Collected only if you grant permission — used for check-in/out and presence verification.

2.4 Push Notifications

  • Push tokens and device identifiers are used to deliver notifications to your device.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve HR features.
  • Send notifications related to your account or app activity.
  • Authenticate sessions and debug performance issues.

4. Sharing & Third Parties

  • We may use service providers (e.g., Firebase, Expo) acting as data processors.
  • We may disclose information if required by law.

We do not sell personal data to advertisers.

5. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary to provide app services or comply with law. You may request deletion.

6. Security

We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is 100% secure. We will follow legal requirements in the case of a breach.

7. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Your Rights & Choices

  • You may control permissions in your device settings.
  • You may request access, changes, or deletion of data.

9. Credentials & Key Security

API keys should be rotated immediately if exposed. Use secure storage for secrets.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as features change. Check the latest date above.
